About

I am a Master’s student in Power Electronics and Drives, working on high-efficiency DC–DC converters, battery management systems, and AI-based State-of-Charge and State-of-Health estimation. I aim to pursue a PhD and contribute long-term, foundational research in energy systems.

Research Interests

  • Bidirectional converters for battery energy storage
  • Battery Management Systems (BMS)
  • AI-based SoC / SoH estimation
  • Control of power electronic systems

Selected Work

  • High-efficiency bidirectional DC–DC converter for battery applications
  • AI-based State-of-Health estimation of Li-ion batteries
  • Presented research at IEEE STPEC 2025 Conference
