Research

My research interests lie at the intersection of power electronics, energy storage, and control, with a particular focus on how these systems evolve over long time horizons and shape future technological infrastructure.

Primary Interests

  • DC–DC converters and high-efficiency power electronic topologies
  • Bidirectional converters for battery energy storage systems
  • Battery Management Systems (BMS)
  • State-of-Charge (SoC) and State-of-Health (SoH) estimation
  • Control of power electronic and energy systems
